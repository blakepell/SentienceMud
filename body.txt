
The first part is called the anchor



Ordering:
none		Just use the name
front		Prefix the body part with FRONT/REAR
side		Prefix the body part with LEFT/RIGHT
order		Prefix the body part with FIRST/SECOND/...




part flags:
upperbody	serves as the main body part
lowerbody	serves as the lower body part
limb		is a limb
grasp		can hold onto things
stance		is used to stand on
circulation
airbreath	needed to breathe in air naturally
waterbreath	needed to breathe underwater naturally
digit		a finger/toe, can wear rings
hear		enables hearing
sight		enables sight
smell		enables smell
vital		a vital body part, if damaged can lead to death
skeleton	forms the skeleton of the body

 APERTURE 		 Unknown effect. An opening in the body, apparently, but no known purpose.
BP 	

    * id 

	The internal object ID of the body part. Unlike many other tokens in the game, the id of the BP does not need to be unique throughout all of the raw object files, though it does need to be unique within the BODY it is in.
BREATHE 		The body part is necessary to breathe. If the body part is destroyed, the creature can suffocate unless it has the NOBREATHE token.
CIRCULATION 		If this body part is destroyed, the creature will bleed to death?
CON 	

    * id 

	The specific body part object ID to which this body part attaches.
CONTYPE 	

    * type 

	The body part token type to which this body part attaches. Note that if the creature has more than one body part with that token type each of them will get its own additional body part attached.
DIGIT 		Is a finger/toe. Unknown effect.
EMBEDDED 		The body part is embedded into the body -- it is external, but is not shown until injured. Compare INTERNAL. Can be gouged out?
FLIER 		The body part is necessary for the creature to fly, and damage to the body part will ground the creature.
GRASP 		The body part can be used to hold items (if the creature has the EQUIPS token) or wrestle.
GUTS 		Can be disembowelled?
HEAD 		Vital bodypart. If severed, creature dies.
HEAR 		Unknown effect. Presumably allows the creature to listen.
INTERNAL 		The body part is an internal organ and is not visible until injured.
JOINT 		Can be broken by wrestling (possibly falling damage in a future version).
LEFT 		The body part is on the left side of the creature and is thus vulnerable to attacks from the left side.
LIMB 		The body part is usable for wrestling (i.e. can grab, lock, choke, etc).
LOWERBODY 		The body part is another part of the "core" of the creature. When the lowerbody body part is destroyed, the creature is bisected.
MOUTH 		Allows the creature to eat?
NERVOUS 		Causes pain when destroyed. Other effects?
RIGHT 		The body part is on the right side of the creature and is thus vulnerable to attacks from the right side.
SIGHT 		Allows creatures to see; creatures without this tag on any part of their bodies cannot see for the purposes of aiming missile weapons (though apparently can find and kill mad adventurers fairly well with melee attacks). May need more testing.
SKELETON 		Prevents game code from removing this bodypart when the creature is converted to skeletal undead.
SMALL 		Unknown effect. Reduces chance to hit?
SMELL 		Unknown effect. Presumably allows creature to smell.
STANCE 		The body part is used to keep the creature standing; injury to the body part will cause the creature to collapse. It also makes the body part act like a foot, and adds toes to the body part. This can be problematic as the body part can be armored by a boot.
THOUGHT 		Necessary for the creature to think. Without a working brain, the creature dies unless it has the NOTHOUGHT Creature Token.
THROAT 		Can be strangled with wrestling to cause winding, unconsciousness, and eventual death. Strangely, even creatures with the NOBREATHE token can be rendered unconscious (but not dead) by strangulation.
UPPERBODY 		The body part is the "core" of the creature; when the upperbody body part is destroyed, the creature is blown apart.
